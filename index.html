Asteroids Game

Fly around in your little Galaxian inspired spaceship! Vrroom Vrroom! :D
Press 'w' to accelerate
Press 'a' and 'd' to rotate
Press 'e' to go into hyperrrrrrspace!
Press the spacebar to shoot a bullet
Watch out for the asteroids! If you collide into one, you'll lose some hp! Don't die newb! :D
